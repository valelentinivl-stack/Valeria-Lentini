
# Valeria Lentini Portfolio

A clean, elegant, and minimal professional portfolio homepage for Valeria Lentini.

## Sections
1. **Hero**: Elegant introduction with mission statement and primary portrait.
2. **Cucina e Rinascita**: Gallery showcasing the therapeutic and creative power of cooking.
3. **Cucina con me**: Interactive local JS section for complete, personalized recipes.
4. **Creativit√† e Tecnologia**: Horizontal gallery with digital copywriting and AI projects.
5. **Contatti**: Professional contact information and social links.

## Recipe Sharing Bot ("Cucina con me")
- **Local Implementation**: Uses a structured JS database for detailed recipes.
- **Content**: Provides Title, Ingredients, and first-person Procedure.
- **Tone of Voice**: Personal, warm, and Sicilian-inspired.

## Media Management
- For the **Cucina** section, images are managed via Imgur URLs or local paths in `/assets/images/cucina/`.
- For the **Digital** section, images and videos can be linked via external URLs (Imgur) or local paths in `/assets/images/digital/`.
- Supported video format: MP4.

## Design Philosophy
The project maintains a warm, sophisticated Sicilian aesthetic with neutral tones and elegant serif typography.

## Tech Stack
- **React 18+** with TypeScript
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **Google Fonts** (Cormorant Garamond & Inter)
